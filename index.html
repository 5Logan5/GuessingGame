Welcome to the Guessing Game

Recent Update: v.14: Added buttons to choose what mode you want to play and bug fixes in mode 3 and now shows what numbers you have recently guessed

Mode 1: There are 20 rounds in this game mode getting harder as the round that your on increases. Don't run out of tries!
Mode 2: There are 10 rounds in this game mode getting harder as the round your on increases (Basically just a faster version of Mode 1). Don't run out of tries!
Mode 3: In this game mode you get to choose how much tries you have. Try to get to the highest round with the amount of tries you have!
